🍷 Privacy Policy

Cavea app · Last updated: April 2026

In short: Cavea does not collect any personal data. Your data stays on your device or in your own Google Drive storage. We have no access to anything.

1. Who are we?

Cavea is a personal wine cellar management app developed by Alain Benard. The app is available on Windows and Android. Source code available on GitHub.

2. Data collected

Cavea does not collect any personal data. There is no user account, no remote server, no analytics, no advertising, no cookies.

The only data processed by the app is:

  • Your wine cellar (bottles, vintages, locations…) — stored in a local SQLite file (cave.db) on your device.
  • Your app preferences (file path, default values) — stored locally in your device's system preferences (SharedPreferences).

3. Google Drive synchronisation (optional)

If you enable Google Drive synchronisation, the app accesses your own Google Drive storage via the official Google API, in a dedicated folder (Cavea/). It only uploads and downloads your cave.db file there.

What this means:

  • Your Google authentication token is stored locally on your device in a secure area (Keychain on Android, Credential Store on Windows). It is never transmitted to any third party other than Google.
  • The app uses the drive.file scope: it can only access files it has itself created in your Drive (the Cavea/ folder, visible in your Google Drive interface). It cannot read or modify anything else in your Drive.
  • No file in your Drive is read or modified by the developer.

Synchronisation is entirely under your control: you can disable it at any time from the app's Settings.

4. Data sharing

No data is shared with third parties. There is no backend server, no remote database, no analytics service. The developer has technically no access to your data.

5. Security

The cave.db file is stored in plain text (unencrypted) on your device. This is a deliberate choice for a personal-use application. If your device is protected by a PIN or password, your data is protected at the same level as all other files on the device.

6. Rights and contact

Since Cavea stores no data server-side, there is no data to view, correct, or delete with the developer. All your data is under your direct control on your device.

For any questions: GitHub Issues.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated with new versions of the app. The date at the top of the page indicates the current version.
