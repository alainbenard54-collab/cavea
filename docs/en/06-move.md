# ↕️ Move a bottle

Change a bottle's location without consuming it.

## Prerequisites

- App open in write mode

## Steps

### 1. Open the action sheet

In the **🍷 Stock** view, tap the row of the bottle you want to move.

### 2. Choose ↕️ Move

Tap **↕️ Move** in the action sheet.

### 3. Enter the new location

The location field offers autocomplete on all locations in your cellar (stock and consumed). Type the first letters to filter suggestions.

**Location format**: `Level1` or `Level1 > Level2 > Level3`

Examples: `Cellar`, `Cellar > Racks > B3`, `Reserve`

You can also type a brand-new location — it will be created automatically.

### 4. Confirm

Tap **Move**. Only the `location` field is updated. The bottle stays in stock.

> **↕️ Move ≠ 🍸 Consume**: changing a location does not mark the bottle as consumed. The exit date is not touched.
