# 🍸 Consume a bottle

Record a bottle as consumed: date, rating, and tasting note.

## Prerequisites

- App open in write mode

## Steps

### 1. Open the action sheet

In the **🍷 Stock** view, tap the row of the bottle you want to consume.

> In read-only mode 🔒, the sheet only shows **ℹ️ View details** and **Close**.

### 2. Choose 🍸 Consume

Tap **🍸 Consume** in the action sheet.

### 3. Fill in the form

| Field | Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Consumption date | Yes | Default: today. Editable via date picker. |
| Rating /10 | No | Score from 0 to 10 |
| Tasting note | No | Your impressions of the wine |

> **Late recording**: if you forget to log a consumption on the day, change the date to reflect when the bottle was actually drunk.

### 4. Confirm

Tap **Confirm**. The bottle disappears from stock and moves to **History** 🕐.

If you made a mistake, you can **reinstate** the bottle from **History** 🕐 (see [10](10-history.md)).
